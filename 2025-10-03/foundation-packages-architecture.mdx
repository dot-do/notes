## Superset Philosophy

### What "Superset" Means

Each .org.ai package is a **superset** of its underlying standard:

1. **100% Compatible** - All standard types included unchanged
2. **AI Extensions** - Additional types for AI use cases
3. **Rapid Evolution** - Can add types faster than standards bodies
4. **Community Driven** - Open to contributions and improvements

### Examples

**schema.org.ai:**
- Standard: Thing, Person, Organization, Place, Event, Product, CreativeWork
- AI Extension: Enhanced JSON-LD types, AI-specific properties, LLM-optimized structures

**gs1.org.ai:**
- Standard: GTIN, GLN, SSCC, EPCIS
- AI Extension: AI-generated product descriptions, ML-enhanced barcodes, automation types

**mdx.org.ai:**
- Standard: MDX, Frontmatter
- AI Extension: AI-generated content types, LLM prompts, agent documentation

### Why Supersets?

1. **Standards Move Slowly** - Official standards take years to update
2. **AI Moves Fast** - AI landscape changes monthly
3. **Compatibility** - Remain compatible while innovating
4. **Adoption** - Easier to adopt (drop-in replacement)
5. **Marketing** - "Superset" signals value-add
